# Hero Image Proposals: Amazon Molly (Fish Cloning)

**Clanek:** `2026-03-11_this-fish-has-cloned-itself-for-100000-years-scientists-just-figured-out-how`
**Typ:** Hero image (3:2)

---

## Vizualni brief (z faze 0)

- **Dominant tone:** scientific
- **Color direction:** Mekke vodni tony — svetla tyrkysova a jasna modr, doplnene neutralnimi sedymi a hnedymi tony ricniho prostredi
- **Key visual moment:** Samicka Amazon molly peri mezi vodnimi rostlinami, zatimco kolem ni plavou jeji geneticky totozne potomky
- **Recommended style:** scientific_illustration
- **Setting:** Sladkovodni mexicka reka nebo akvarium s prirozenym osvetlenim

## Ton a paleta

**Ton:** Scientific
- Osvetleni: Rovnomerne studiove, rim light pro separaci
- Kompozice: Symetricke, diagramaticky presne, centrovane
- Prompt fragment: `clean even studio lighting with subtle rim light, neutral blue-white palette, symmetrical centered composition, sharp focus throughout, clinical precision`

**Paleta (Ocean / morsky zivot):**
- Primarni: Tyrkysova `#006994`
- Sekundarni: Tmava modr `#2E4057`
- Akcent: Koralova `#FF6B35`
- Pozadi: Hluboka modr `#0A1628`

**Narativni oblouk:** Objev (tmave neurcite → jasne zarive) — vedecky prulon v pochopeni klonovaciho mechanismu.

---

## Varianta A — Metaforicka kolaz

**Nazev:** Zrcadleni bez otce

**Popis kompozice:** Soustava identickych ryb Amazon molly usporadanych v zrcadlovem vzoru, kazda ryba je presnou kopii ostatnich. Uprostred kompozice jemne prozarovana spirala DNA s vyraznenymi useky genkonverze. Kontrast organicke prirody (zive ryby) a abstraktni genetiky.

**Prompt:**
```
Hero image for genetics article. Identical silvery Amazon molly fish arranged in mirror-symmetrical pattern around a softly glowing DNA helix with highlighted gene conversion segments, scientific illustration style, clean even studio lighting with cyan rim light, centered symmetrical composition, turquoise and deep blue palette with coral accents --ar 3:2
```
(46 slov)

**Ton:** Scientific | **Paleta:** Tyrkysova + hluboka modr + koralovy akcent

---

## Varianta B — Editorial Hero Shot

**Nazev:** Matka sta generaci

**Popis kompozice:** Stredni podvodni zaber jedne samicky Amazon molly v ostrosti, obklopene rozmazanymi siluetami geneticky identickych potomku ve vsech smerech. Prirozene sladkovodni svetlo pronika shora, vytvareni kausticky vzor na pisecnem dne. Ryba se diva primo k divakovi.

**Prompt:**
```
Hero image for cloning biology article. Single female Amazon molly fish in sharp focus facing camera, surrounded by dozens of blurred identical offspring in turquoise freshwater, natural light filtering from above creating caustic ripple patterns on sandy riverbed, medium close-up underwater shot, cool blue-white palette with warm sand tones --ar 3:2
```
(49 slov)

**Ton:** Scientific | **Paleta:** Modro-bila + pieskove tony

---

## Varianta C — Symbolicka scena

**Nazev:** Sto tisic let bez zmen

**Popis kompozice:** Siroky podvodni zaber mexicke sladkovodni reky s hustou vegetaci. V popredi plave jedna Amazon molly, za ni se v hloubce linearne radi desitky dalsich identickych ryb — mizici do modrave mlhy jako "casova osa" klonu sahajici do minulosti. Kompozice naznacuje nekonecnou sekvenci generaci.

**Prompt:**
```
Hero image for evolutionary biology article. Wide underwater view of lush Mexican freshwater river, single Amazon molly fish in foreground with dozens of identical fish receding in linear formation into misty blue depth suggesting deep time, scientific illustration style, even ambient aquatic light, symmetrical depth composition, turquoise-to-deep-indigo gradient palette --ar 3:2
```
(48 slov)

**Ton:** Scientific | **Paleta:** Tyrkysova → hluboke indigo gradient

## Thumbnail Validace (100x67px test)

| Rozpoznatelny ve 100x67px | WARN — DNA detail se ztrati | OK — ryba v centru citelna | WARN — perspektivni rada splyne |
| Za 0.5s jasne o cem je | OK — ryby + DNA = genetika | OK — ryba s kopiemi = klony | RIZIKO — muze vypadat jako bezny podvodni zaber |

| Odpovida ton a paleta | OK | OK | OK |

**Doporuceni:** Varianta **B** je nejsilnejsi pro thumbnail — jasny fokus na jednu rybu, obklopenou klony. Varianta A je vizualne nejzajimavejsi, ale riskuje ztraceni detailu DNA v malych rozmerech. Varianta C je narativne nejsilnejsi, ale v thumbnails slabsi.
